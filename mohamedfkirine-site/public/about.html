Research

I study deterministic and stochastic evolution equations, focusing on well-posedness, stability, admissibility, and controllability of infinite-dimensional systems.

Interests

  • Functional analysis & operator theory (C₀-semigroups)
  • Control theory of infinite-dimensional systems
  • Stochastic analysis in Hilbert spaces

Current position
