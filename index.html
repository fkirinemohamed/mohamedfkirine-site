About

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Systems Theory Research Group at the Mathematics Research Centre, Tampere University. My work combines analytic and probabilistic methods to study stochastic and deterministic PDEs, including questions of well-posedness, stability, and controllability.

PhD in Applied Mathematics (Ibn Zohr University, 2023).

Research Interests

Functional analysis & operator theory
One-parameter semigroup theory; perturbations; admissibility.
Control of infinite-dimensional systems
Stability and controllability for PDE models.
Stochastic analysis in infinite dimensions
SPDEs, boundary noise, regularity and long-time behavior.

Research

Stochastic PDEs with boundary noise
Well-posedness and regularity for evolution equations driven by boundary perturbations.
Semigroup methods & perturbation theory
Admissibility, generator perturbations, and applications to PDE models.
Stability of infinite-dimensional systems
Polynomial stability and related robustness questions (e.g., structural models).
Control under constraints
Controllability questions with positivity constraints in structured population models.
